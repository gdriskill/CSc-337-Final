How to Play

Your goal in this game is to explore and interact with your environment in order to solve puzzles and escape.
You have three types of actions to play the game: look, interact and move. First, click on the type of action you wish to perform on the top bar. Once your choice is highlighted red, then you can click spots on the game image to do that action on a specific item in the game. Your mouse cursor type will change when hovering over top a valid place to click.
After clicking somewhere on the screen, a text response will appear on the right side of the screen. White text indicates descriptions, red text indicates noises and green text indicates actions.
Through out the game, you will acquire objects. These objects will be placed in your inventory. Images of the objects you possess will appear in the middle section on screen. To use an object in your inventory, first click on the object then click the area of game image you wish to use that object in.
